Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ParseFlow, a data parsing and visualization tool developed by Stephan El Khoury. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data You Upload

ParseFlow processes the following types of data that you voluntarily upload:

  • JSON files and their contents
  • CSV files and their data
  • Excel files (.xlsx, .xls) and their data
  • Any metadata associated with uploaded files

2.2 Local Storage

ParseFlow stores data locally in your browser using:

  • localStorage for parsed data and application settings
  • sessionStorage for temporary authentication state
  • No server-side data storage

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Parsing and analyzing uploaded files
  • Generating visualizations and statistics
  • Providing data management features (cards, filters, search)
  • Maintaining application functionality and user preferences

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data processing occurs locally in your browser
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • User authentication is handled locally
  • You maintain full control over your data

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as follows:

  • Uploaded data persists in your browser's local storage until manually cleared
  • You can delete all data using the "Clear All Data" function
  • Data is automatically cleared when you clear your browser's storage
  • No data backup or recovery services are provided

6. Third-Party Services

ParseFlow uses the following third-party libraries:

  • Chart.js for data visualization
  • SheetJS (XLSX) for Excel file processing
  • Font Awesome for icons
  • Google Fonts for typography

These services may have their own privacy policies and terms of use.

7. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access all your stored data through the application interface
  • Export your data in JSON format
  • Delete all your data at any time
  • Control what data you upload and process

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
